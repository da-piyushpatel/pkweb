Maths Books IIT-JEE

Engineers Love Maths! Right

Cengage Written by G Tewani. It has sufficent problems taking your level from zero to Hero. You can solve MCQs for mains and subjective questions for advanced point of view. The main reason you should have that book is because it has many questions from different modules from different coaching classes across India. If you can solve each question from cengage maths you are unbeatable, but solving so many questions requires time and effort which you hardly have during your prepration. So I would recommmend everyone to atleast solve the solved examples before moving on to the questions Both books the one for Mains and other one for advanced would definitly help you to ace your preprations. If you have this book you don't need any other book. Checkout Cengage For JEE Mains | Checkout Cengage For JEE Advanced



R.D. Sharma Objective Old but Gold that's all I can say, this book contains enough quality questions for you to practice.



Wiley's Maths many people ask me which book to buy when it comes to theory and detailed explanation of concepts. Well this is the book. There are many good solved examples detailed steps.

Wiley's Maths - Alzebra | Wiley's Maths - Trignometry | Wiley's Maths - Calculas | Wiley's Maths - Geometry | Wiley's Maths |



Mathematics is all about practice you don't need to watch a lot of lectures to get your concepts clear but you need to solve a lot of quality questions.

Past Year Papers IIT-JEE Solving Past Year Papers cannot be ignored at all, so you need to have this book.

Play With Grahs A major portion of Maths is covered by graphs, weather it is Quadratic Equations, Alzebra, Calculas, Trignometry most of the times it is Graphs. And the fun part is most people ignore it. This book will help you to understand and master the concepts and implementation of graphs.
