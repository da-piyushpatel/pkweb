NCERT and Reference Books for IIT-JEE

It is small but significant enough to shake your rank

Chemistry NCERT NCERT Chemistry is one of the best book you can get for JEE Mains & Advanced. For JEE Mains this book is like bible, almost every question the paper asks comes directly from this book. For JEE Advanced this book still serves as a good foundation to help in building furthur concepts. Many times it may happen that you get questions directly handpicked and dropped into Advanced Paper, so I would say learn each and every line and paragraph from this book. NCERT Physics When it comes to physics NCERTs have a decent content to start learning. If you are a CBSE Board student then this is a must to have book for you, but for people from other boards I would say you can still do pretty well without this book, just focus on whatever board level book you have.

Click here to buy Physics NCERT Class 11 | Click here to buy Physics NCERT Class 12



NCERT Maths For Maths, NCERTs have a good number of problems to solve and get a good hold on concepts. Again if you are a CBSE Board student then this is a must to have book for you, but for people from other boards I would say you can still do pretty well without this book, just focus on whatever board level book you have. In fact I would advice you to use RD Sharma than NCERT Maths.

NCERTs are small and difficult to comprehend easily. I would advice to get some additional books to make your life easier and master NCERT.

Past Year Papers IIT-JEE Solving Past Year Papers cannot be ignored at all, so you need to have this book. Use this book to analyze your performance from time to time.

NCERT at Your Fingertips This is the book I recommend each and every student to have, weather you are from CBSE Board or not, preparing for JEE Mains or Advanced, Because this book has got some really good quality questions which you can practice and master NCERT. You know what weightage NCERT has on your scores, and any additional help is benificial to have.

NCERT Exampler Problems If you are still not satisfied with your prepration and want an extra kick to your preprations, you can get NCERT Exampler to practive more challenging questions.



Modern ABC Books These set of books helped me a lot during my preprations. The detailed theory and enough number of solved and unsolved questions helped me to self prepare and ace Physics without and external help. Physics never seem tough for me after solving this book. Being a ICSE Board student, our class book was different but I hardly used to solve it's questions. After solving examples and questions from ABC Physics and Chemistry books, I was able to solve most of the Boards and JEE Mains questions. Alternatively you can go for Pradeep Fundamentals of Physics it has similar content.
